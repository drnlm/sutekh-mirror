The Sutekh Manual

Introduction and Important Concepts

Introduction

Sutekh is a card management program for Vampire: The Eternal Struggle, a Collectable Card Game published by White Wolf.

Important Concepts

The Card Collection

Sutekh distinguishes between two types of cards: Abstract Cards, and Physical Cards.

Abstract Cards represent a particular card in the published card list, for instance "Aire of Elation" or the Salubri vampire "Saulot". Physical Cards are copies of the cards in your collection, which might be a copy of "Aire of Elation" from the Camarilla Edition expansion. Abstract Cards are intended to describe the non-physical aspects of the card, such as discipline requirements and card text, while physical cards capture details such as expansion, and the number in your collection.

Much of Sutekh is involved with working with your collection of cards, which will all be physical cards. The list of all possible cards is also available as the White wolf card list.

Card Sets

In addition to the lists of cards, Sutekh allows you to create sets of either Abstract or Physical Cards. A set of Physical Cards must be a subset of your card collection. These sets can be used to represent any subdivision of interest. Creating decks is the most common use of card sets, but they can also be used to represent sets of cards you are interested in trading, different ways of subdividing your collection (in storage, available for use, etc.)

Abstract Card Sets are useful when the physical properties aren't important - speculative deck ideas, cards that one wishes to acquire, and so forth. Physical Card Sets are subsets of the collection and thus can be used to represent constructed decks, a cards available for trade, and so forth.
